Chicken Stir-Fry

Description

A quick, colorful stir-fry with tender chicken and crisp veggies, tossed in a savory sauce — great for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced thin
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Cooked rice, to serve

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over high heat.
  2. Add sliced chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add garlic, then vegetables. Stir-fry for 3–4 minutes until just tender.
  4. Return chicken to the pan. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and sesame oil.
  5. Stir everything together and cook for another 1–2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot over cooked rice.
