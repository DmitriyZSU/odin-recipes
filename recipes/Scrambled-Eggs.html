Scrambled Eggs

Description

Soft, fluffy scrambled eggs made with just a few ingredients â€” ideal for breakfast or a light meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk (optional for creaminess)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Chopped chives or herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl, add milk (if using), and whisk until well blended.
  2. Heat a nonstick pan over low-medium heat and melt the butter.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture.
  4. Stir gently with a spatula, scraping the pan as the eggs set.
  5. Cook until eggs are just set but still slightly soft.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and top with herbs.
